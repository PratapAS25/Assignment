Our Story

Founded in 1998 by John and Sarah Thompson, EliteBuild has grown from a small local contractor to one of the region's most trusted construction firms. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction has remained unchanged throughout our journey.

Our Approach

We believe in transparent communication, meticulous planning, and using only the highest quality materials. Every project is treated with the same level of care, whether it's a small renovation or a large commercial development.
